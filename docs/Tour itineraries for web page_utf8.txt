Tour #1.

*5 Day Sri Lanka Highlights Tour*  
Perfect if it’s your first time + you’re flying in/out of Colombo. Mix of culture, nature, tea, and beach.

*Day 1: Colombo → Sigiriya/Dambulla*  
- Morning: Pick up from Colombo, drive 4-5 hrs to Cultural Triangle
- Afternoon: Dambulla Cave Temple - huge Buddha statues + cave paintings
- Evening: Check in near Sigiriya, relax. Sunset view from Pidurangala if you’ve got energy
- *Stay*: Sigiriya/Dambulla area

*Day 2: Sigiriya → Kandy*  
- Early morning: Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress. 1200 steps but the view + frescoes are worth it
- Afternoon: Drive 3 hrs to Kandy. Visit Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
- Evening: Kandy Lake walk + cultural dance show
- *Stay*: Kandy

*Day 3: Kandy → Nuwara Eliya*  
- Morning: Peradeniya Botanical Gardens + Ceylon tea tasting
- Afternoon: Drive to “Little England” Nuwara Eliya. 
- Evening: Gregory Lake + cold mountain air
- *Stay*: Nuwara Eliya

*Day 4: Nuwara Eliya → Ella → Mirissa/Galle*  
- Morning drive to Ella visit Nine Arch Bridge for that classic train shot (depening on train time)
- Afternoon: Long drive 5-6 hrs down to south coast. Galle Fort if you stop there
- Evening: Hit the beach at Mirissa/Unawatuna, seafood dinner
- *Stay*: Mirissa or Unawatuna

*Day 5: South Coast → Colombo*  
- Morning: Turtle hatchery in Kosgoda or whale watching in Mirissa if it’s season Dec-Apr
- Afternoon: Drive back to Colombo 2.5-3 hrs. Last min shopping at Dutch Hospital or Pettah Market
- Evening: Fly out / end tour

*Note: This is just a guideline only, and we will assist you to your organise  your trip according to your requirements.




Tour ＃2

7-day cultural tour of Sri Lanka — hits the big heritage spots without rushing you:

*Day 1: Colombo / Negambo → Sigiriya*  
- Arrive, drive to Cultural Triangle ∼4hrs  
- Afternoon Evening: Minneriya or Echo Park  (watch elephannt gathering) + traditional dinner in Habarana ,  
*Stay*: Sigiriya/Habarana area

*Day 2: Sigiriya & Dambulla*  
- Sunrise climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress - UNESCO site, ancient palace + frescoes  
- Afternoon: Dambulla Cave Temple with 150+ Buddha statues  
*Stay*: Sigiriya/ Habarana

*Day 3: Polonnaruwa*  
- Full day cycling/exploring ancient city ruins - Royal Palace, Gal Vihara Buddha statues  
- Evening: Back to Sigiriya/ Habarana  Aurweda Spa (relax) 
*Stay*: Sigiriya/ Habarana

*Day 4: Kandy*  
- Drive 3.5hrs via spice garden in Matale  
- Visit Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic  
- Evening: Kandy cultural dance show , enjoy a walk along the Kandy lake 
*Stay*: Kandy

*Day 5: Kandy → Nuwara Eliya*  
- Morning: Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya  
- Tea factory tour + tasting  
*Stay*: Nuwara Eliya "Little England"

*Day 6: Nuwara Eliya → Ella*  
- Morning: Horton Plains + World’s End viewpoint if you’re up early  
- Drive to Ella 1.5hrs  
- Sunset at Nine Arch Bridge  
*Stay*: Ella

*Day 7: Ella → Galle*  
- Morning: Little Adam’s Peak hike 45min

Tour #5

*7-Day Culture + Wildlife Tour Sri Lanka*

*Day 1: Colombo → Negombo*  
Arrive, relax at beach. Visit St. Mary’s Church + fish market. Overnight Negombo.

*Day 2: Negombo → Sigiriya*  
Drive 4hrs. Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress “Lion Rock” + see 5th century frescoes. Sunset view. Overnight Sigiriya.

*Day 3: Polonnaruwa + Minneriya Safari*  
Morning: Explore ancient city of Polonnaruwa - UNESCO ruins, Gal Vihara Buddha statues.  
Evening: Minneriya safari for “The Gathering” of elephants Jul-Sep. Other months = Kaudulla. Overnight Sigiriya.

*Day 4: Sigiriya → Kandy*  
Stop at Dambulla Cave Temple on way. Afternoon: Kandy city tour + Temple of Sacred Tooth Relic. Cultural dance show in evening. Overnight Kandy.

*Day 5: Kandy → Yala*  
Morning train to Ella for tea plantation views, or direct drive 6hrs to Yala. Evening safari: Leopards, elephants, crocs. Overnight Yala.

*Day 6: Yala Full Day Safari*  
Early morning + evening game drives. Yala has highest leopard density in the world. Also sloth bears, jackals, 200+ birds. Overnight Yala.

*Day 7: Yala → Galle → Colombo*  
Drive via Galle. Stop at Galle Fort Dutch colonial town + lighthouse. Continue to Colombo for departure/flight. (End of tour)

*Note: This is just a guideline only, and we will assist you to your organise your trip according to your requirements.
