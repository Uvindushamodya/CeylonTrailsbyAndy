Cultural Triangle

Ancient Heritage Tour

Journey back in time and discover the ancient kingdoms of Sri Lanka. This tour takes you through the magnificent Sigiriya Rock Fortress, the well-preserved ruins of Polonnaruwa, and the stunning cave temples of Dambulla. Experience the rich history and vibrant culture of the island.

  • Sigiriya Lion Rock Climb
  • Polonnaruwa Ancient City Tour

Southern Wilds

Yala Leopard Safari

$320 per person / 3 days

Immerse yourself in the wild beauty of southern Sri Lanka. This thrilling safari adventure takes you deep into Yala National Park, home to one of the highest densities of leopards in the world, as well as majestic elephants, sloth bears, and diverse birdlife.

  • Full Day Jeep Safari in Yala
  • Bundala Bird Sanctuary Visit
  • Relaxing on Mirissa Beach

Tea & Train Odyssey

$600 per person / 7 days

Escape to the misty mountains of Sri Lanka. Wind your way through rolling tea estates on one of the world's most scenic train rides. Hike to spectacular viewpoints like Little Adam's Peak and witness the roaring waterfalls that dot the central highlands.
